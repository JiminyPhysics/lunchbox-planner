# 🍱 My Lunchbox Planner

A fun, interactive school lunchbox planner for kids — built as a single HTML file, no installs needed.

**➡️ [Open the app](https://jiminyphysics.github.io/lunchbox-planner/)**

---

## What it does

Kids plan their school lunches for the whole week by tapping sections of a virtual bento-style lunchbox and choosing from a colourful food picker. Parents get a ready-made shopping list to copy straight into their phone.

### Features

- **b.box-style lunchbox** with five sections: Main Meal, Veggie, Fruit, Protein Snack, and Treat
- **Monday–Friday day selector** — plan the whole week, one day at a time
- **Two-step picker for sandwiches and wraps** — choose the filling (cheese, ham, vegemite / chicken, ham & cheese)
- **Animated monster mascot** that reacts as the lunchbox fills up, with encouraging messages
- **Celebration screen** with confetti when a day's lunchbox is complete, plus a one-tap "Next day →" button
- **Week overview strip** showing all five days at a glance with food emoji previews
- **Smart shopping list** that tallies ingredients across the whole week — bread slices counted, not just "sandwiches" — and copies as clean plain text (no emojis) ready to paste into any shopping app

---

## How to use

1. Click a day button on the left (Mon–Fri)
2. Tap any section of the lunchbox to open the food picker
3. Tap a food to select it — it fills in immediately
4. Repeat for all five sections
5. Hit the **🛒 Make My Shopping List!** button when the week is planned
6. Tap **📋 Copy to Clipboard** and paste into your notes or shopping app

## Technical notes

- Pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript — no frameworks, no dependencies, no build step
- Single file: `lunchbox-planner.html`
- Works in any modern browser on desktop or mobile
- No data is sent anywhere — everything stays in the browser
